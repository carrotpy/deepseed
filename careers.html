Robotics Instructor

Full-Time Actively Hiring

Lead hands-on robotics workshops for students aged 7–18+. You'll design lesson plans, conduct live coding sessions with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and Python, and mentor the next generation of builders and inventors.

Arduino Raspberry Pi Python Scratch PictoBlox

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and deliver structured robotics curricula for multiple age groups
  • Conduct hands-on lab sessions with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Scratch, and Python
  • Assess student progress and provide personalised mentorship
  • Create engaging project-based learning experiences
  • Collaborate with the R&D team to integrate real-world applications into lessons

Qualifications

  • B.E/B.Tech in Electronics, Mechatronics, Computer Science or related field
